# LP-2860: Fund Management Index

## Abstract

This LP serves as the index and entry point for all fund management, treasury, and institutional investment specifications in the Lux ecosystem.

## Motivation

Institutional investors, fund managers, and DAOs need clear standards for:
- Managing on-chain treasuries
- Operating investment funds
- Implementing fund-of-funds structures
- Yield optimization strategies
