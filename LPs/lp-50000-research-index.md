# LP-50000: Research Papers Index

## Abstract

This LP serves as the master index for all research papers, academic work, experimental protocols, and frontier technology explorations in the Lux ecosystem.

## Motivation

Separating research from production specifications enables:
- Clear distinction between implemented and theoretical work
- Academic rigor without implementation pressure
- Space for experimental concepts
- Community contribution pathways
- Future technology roadmapping

### Research Process

1. **Proposal**: Submit draft to 50xxx range
2. **Review**: Peer review by research committee
3. **Publication**: Finalize as Informational LP
4. **Implementation**: If viable, create Standards Track LP in appropriate range

### Academic Partnerships

Research LPs may be co-authored with:
- University research groups
- Industry research labs
- Independent researchers
- Security audit firms

## Security Considerations

Research papers should:
- Clearly label theoretical vs. practical content
- Disclose any security assumptions
- Not include production-ready code without audit
- Follow responsible disclosure for vulnerabilities
